## Scroll Through LinkedIn or GitHub for a Bit, and You'll Start Noticing a Pattern

People quietly building GenAI projects, writing about prompt engineering, fine-tuning models, or shipping AI tools — often without much fanfare. They're out there. You just have to know where to look.

What if finding and organizing those profiles was actually worth something?

That's the idea behind Kactii's latest initiative. Let's break down what it really involves.

## So, What Does This Actually Involve?

This isn't about creating a profile of your own — it's about going out and discovering *other* people's profiles. Specifically, LinkedIn or GitHub links belonging to people whose work is related to GenAI (generative AI): think engineers, researchers, hobbyists, or students actively working with AI tools and models.

You can do this search from your phone. In simple terms: you search, you find, you collect the link, you submit it. That's the whole loop.

Why does this matter? Because spotting genuine GenAI talent takes a bit of curiosity and effort — and Kactii wants to reward the people willing to put in that legwork.

## The Reward: 250 INR in Kactii Credits

For your effort in sourcing these profiles, Kactii credits your account with 250 INR worth of Kactii Credits. Think of these as in-app currency — not cash you withdraw, but value you can put toward things that genuinely help your career journey.

A bit of research and curiosity today unlocks options you can use whenever you're ready.

## What Can You Actually Do With These Credits?

This is where it gets genuinely useful. Your credits aren't just a gimmick — they open up three real paths:

**Mock Interviews.** Nervous about an upcoming interview? Use your credits toward a mock interview session on Kactii. It's a low-pressure way to practice answering tough questions, get feedback, and walk into the real thing with more confidence.

**Kactii Swags.** Sometimes it's nice to have something tangible to show for your effort. Redeem credits for Kactii merchandise — a small, fun reward for taking the first step.

**Tech Gifts.** Prefer something practical? Save up your credits and put them toward tech gifts like headsets — the kind of gear that's genuinely useful for calls, interviews, or everyday work.

## A Quick Note on Balance

If you're collecting profiles from your phone, worth saying clearly: this isn't about staying glued to your screen searching all day. Fit it into spare moments — a commute, a coffee break, whenever it's convenient. It's meant to add to your day, not take it over.
